Game Instructions

General Rules:

  • The game consists of 14 tiles, divided into 5 groups: 4 groups of 3 tiles and 1 group of 2 tiles.
  • Form patterns like Shunzi (sequential tiles of the same type), Kezi (three identical tiles), and Duizi (two identical tiles).
  • Achieve Hu by forming Kezi or Shunzi in all 4 groups of 3 tiles and Duizi in the final group of 2 tiles.

Examples:

  • Shunzi: 1条, 2条, 3条 (Sequential tiles of the same type)
  • Kezi: 3万, 3万, 3万 (Three identical tiles)
  • Duizi: 5筒, 5筒 (Two identical tiles)
  • Hu: 4 valid groups of Shunzi/Kezi and 1 group of Duizi
